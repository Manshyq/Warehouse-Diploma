Questions

How to create an account?

+

Open the Warehouse app to get started and follow the steps. Warehouse doesn’t charge a fee to create or maintain your Tradebase account.

How do I edit product information?

+

You can edit product information by navigating to the product page, selecting the product you wish to edit, and clicking the 'Edit' button. Make sure to save any changes before exiting the page.

Is it possible to delete a product from the inventory?

+

Yes, you can delete a product by selecting the product on the product page and clicking the 'Delete' button. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

How can I add a QR code to my product?

+

To add a QR code, go to the product's details page and select 'Add QR Code'. Follow the prompts to generate a new QR code for your product.

Where can I find customer contact information?

+

Customer contact information can be found on the Customers page. Select a customer to view their full profile, including contact details.

How do I edit customer information?

+

Edit customer information by selecting a customer on the Customers page and clicking 'Edit'. Remember to save any changes to update the customer's profile.

What are the benefits of buying a premium update, and how can I purchase it?

+

A premium update provides additional features such as advanced analytics, priority customer support, and more. To buy a premium update, go to your account settings and select 'Upgrade to Premium'.

How can I cancel my premium subscription?

+

To cancel your premium subscription, please go to your account settings and select 'Subscription'. Click on 'Cancel Subscription' and follow the steps to confirm cancellation.
